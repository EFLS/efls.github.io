About

I am a cultural sociologist and a Human-Computer Interaction researcher at Mintlab, KU Leuven. From 2012 until 2018, I was teaching assistant in general and theoretical sociology at the University of Antwerp, where I earned a doctoral degree in sociology with a dissertation on debt, indebtedness and debt collection. Outside of my academic interests I enjoy exploring emacs’ text editing capacities.
