About

I am a cultural sociologist and a Human-Computer Interaction researcher at Mintlab, KU Leuven. From 2012 until 2018, I was teaching assistant in general and theoretical sociology at the University of Antwerp, where I am finalizing a doctoral dissertation on debt, indebtedness and debt collection.
